Shipping Policy

We aim to have all products processed and shipped out within 3 business days of full payment on orders of five or less.  On orders with quantities over five we will have the order processed and shipped out within five business days.  All shipping is conducted through either Fed Ex or UPS on orders of five or more. The customer will receive tracking information on products upon shipping.


Return Policy

We want you to be fully satisfied with every item that you purchase from The Plumber's Choice. If you are not satisfied with an item that you have purchased, you may return the item up to thirty days after the original purchase date as long as the item has not been altered or shows signs of use.

The item must be returned in good condition, in the original packaging and with all paperwork, parts and accessories to insure full credit. Customer is responsible for paying for shipping when returning the product unless item is damaged upon recieving.

Call us 888-989-4260 for a RMA authorization.
