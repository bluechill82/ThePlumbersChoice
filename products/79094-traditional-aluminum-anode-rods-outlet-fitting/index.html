Blue Lightning Rods Traditional Aluminum Anode Rod
Residental with Outlet Fitting

Do you use Well Water?
Do you use Water Softener?
Do you go Extended Periods of Time without
using your water heater?

If you answered yes to any of these questions than the Aluminium Anode Rod is definitely for you. Generally, magnesium is the best choice for residential water heaters but under the conditions above exceptions should be made.

The Traditional Aluminium Anode Rod has a length of 42 inches. This traditional aluminium model of anode rod comes as a solid straight single piece of aluminium with a steel core and outlet fitting. This rod should provide at least two years of protection. You should always conduct annual inspections and flush your water tank on a yearly basis.
